About Oxygen Gym Located in the heart of Veluthamanal, Karunagappally, Oxygen Gym is a premier 5-star fitness destination designed for those who demand excellence. Our state-of-the-art, fully air-conditioned facility combines a high-energy "billionaire vibe" with elite equipment, creating a modern and motivating environment for every member of the community.

Whether your goal is weight management, professional bodybuilding, or overall wellness, our expert, supportive trainers provide the personalized guidance you need to succeed. We pride ourselves on maintaining a clean, inclusive, and professional space where beginners and athletes alike can breathe life into their fitness journey and achieve lasting transformations.
